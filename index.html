How it works

Every signal stays visible.

1

Observed evidence

RiftGG lane matchups, RankedWR Diamond+ role strength, and statsWR historical comparison remain separate evidence components.

2

Whole-draft fit

Every selected enemy and ally contributes to labeled enemy-response and ally-synergy estimates using official champion classes and ability signals.

3

Transparent ranking

Available components are combined into an evidence score; the full breakdown and data coverage are shown for every recommendation.

Lane matchup, role strength, and historical change are published statistics. Enemy response and ally synergy are estimates from official champion classes and ability descriptions—not observed composition win rates or a win probability.
